30 days of Web Dev!

I wasn't really good at coding. Maybe I am still not , so when I heard about DSC's web development course , I was a little hesitant about enrolling for this course. But with a little push from myself and a couple of friends , I joined the course.

I love web design! Not really sure why , but I just do. With the material I've learnt from DSC's course , my skills have increased ten-fold. Of course , I still have a long way to go , but at least I've come much more further from where I first started! So Thank you DSC for this wonderful course that you've provided us with!
